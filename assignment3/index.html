Introduction

This webpage is an "interactive storytelling website" where you can choose how your story unfolds.

Become the main character of the story by choosing the options provided as the story unfolds!

Please note that if you do not select an option, it will be automatically selected after 30 seconds.

If you're ready to start the story, scroll and enjoy!!

* This story is a retelling of Aesop's fable, 'The Fox and the Stork'.
