Introduction

This webpage is an “interactive storytelling website” where you can choose how your story unfolds. As the story progresses, choose the options provided to become the main character of the story! You can advance the story by scrolling, and click on the given option buttons to unfold the story in the direction you want. This story is 'The Koala and the Platypus', a retelling of Aesop's fable 'The Fox and the Stork'. Experience a short and simple story.

The following precautions are required for smooth progress:
  • Contains many images. Please start after the webpage is fully loaded.
  • The story can be progressed by scrolling. Don't scroll back while the story progresses.

    • If you reverse the scroll, code may malfunction.

  • If a scroll button is placed, you should click on the corresponding option. Please stop scrolling for a moment

    • where the buttons are placed. Also, if there is no button, you have to scroll to progress through the story.

  • Includes a function to automatically select options to progress the story. Please note that if you do not

    • select an option, an option will be automatically selected at random. But don't worry! Auto-selection will

    take effect after 15 seconds. You have plenty of time to think about it, so you can choose at your leisure.

  • If you don't like the option you selected, try selecting the option again with the back button. There is a button

    • on the story that allows you to go back to the previous page. (It only exists in the first part of the story executed

    after selecting an option.) It's located at the top left of the page, so don't worry if you make the wrong selection.

    If you're ready, scroll to start the story
